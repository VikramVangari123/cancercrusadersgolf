PAST EVENTS

CCIC 2018

Cancer Crusaders Invitation Golf Cup, for it's 5th edition surpassed all records by hosting a whopping 315 players. Further, for the first time, everyone enjoyed a parallel Mixed Reality Golf Championship which was more fun with fewer rules and more pun & high technology! Our host venue for three editions now - the beautiful Hyderabad Golf Club ensured the delight expected by our special invited guests was delivered without a hitch. Actress Kaajal Agarwal was the chief guest for the Pre-tournament press meet and inaugurated the tournament while taking a shot at the sport herself.

Needless to say, the most anticipated event after the competitive game-play was the Celebrity Golf Playoff along with the awards ceremony hosted by our trusted and consistent contributor Novotel HICC. Audience were enthralled when the celebrity players were asked to drive the ball into a net between marked pillars - ensuing into a competitive and greatly entertaining session. Ms. Sania Mirza, Ace Tennis Player; Ms. Ananda Shankar Jayant, Classical Dancer; Smt. Shikha Goel, IPS, DIG, CISF; Ms. Amrapali Kata, IAS; Dr. P. Shashikala Reddy, Professor of Micro for Telangana State; Ms. Saptala Reddy, M.D., Cybercity Builders & Developers; Ms. Vanaja Banagiri, Former Editor Hyderabad Times; Ms. Shilpa Reddy, Designer; Ms. Shalini Vadnikatti, Model & Cine Actress; Ms. Ritika Singh, Actress; Ms. Hebah Patel; Actress; Mr. Konda Vishweshwar Reddy, M.P. were the participants for the night game in HICC along with some dancing, cocktails and sumptuous dinner. One thing on everyone's mind was the arduous two year wait for the 10 year Anniversary Edition coming up in 2020!

CCIC 2016

The 4th edition of the Cancer Crusaders Invitation Golf Cup was held at the resplendent Hyderabad Golf Club! Actress Tamannaah Bhatia was the chief guest for the Pre-tournament press meet and inaugurated the tournament while taking a shot at the sport herself.

The two day tournament witnessed the participation of about 210 passionate golfers from all over the country. The most awaited moment of the series was the Celebrity Golf Play Off and prize distribution ceremony held at the exquisite Novotel HICC.

Actress Rakul Preet Singh was the chief guest of the play off and being a golfer herself, though she could not participate in the competition, she lent a helping hand to all the established ladies on the dais namely Lakshmi Manchu (Actress), Madhu Shalini (Actress), Shilpa Reddy (Former Mrs. India and fashion designer), Sangita Reddy (Executive Director, Apollo Hospitals), Swati Lakra (IPS), Shashikala Reddy (Doctor), Christina Z Chongthu (IAS), Sapthala Reddy (Entrepreneur), Anupama Sachdeva (Fashion designer and stylist).

CCIC 2014

Cancer Crusaders Invitation Cup 2014, held at the beautiful Hyderabad Golf Club overlooking Golconda Fort, participated by 210 golfers. The players competed over the course of two days being scored by the Stableford system.

The golf tournament was inaugurated by the internationally acclaimed tennis players Sania Mirza and Bethanie Mattek-Sands.

The tournament was followed by a Celebrity Playoff cocktail dinner at Novotel Convention Centre. The famous couple Mr and Mrs Akkineni and Amala Nagarjuna showed their support to the Cancer Crusaders cause by participating in the Celebrity Playoff together with such celebrities and opinion leaders like actors Ms Samantha and Ms Madhu Shalini, fashion designer Ms Asmita Marwa, the captain of Indian Women's Cricket team Ms Mithali Raj, fitness trainer Ms Dinaz Vervatwala, the executive director of Apollo Life Ms Upasana Kamineni and Dr. P. Shashikala Reddy.

CCIC 2012

Putting the cause for raising cancer awareness above all, 162 golfers participated in the Cancer Crusaders Invitation Cup-2012 at the EMAAR MGF Boulder Hills Golf Club on March 3rd and 4th. Five clubs from Hyderabad and six players from Delhi and Bangalore have participated at the fundraiser for CURE Foundation.

The celebrity Akkineni Nagarjuna was hosted at the event which was organized for the purpose of raising awareness regarding cancer.

The foundation by then had worked with 500 underprivileged children to provide them with cancer treatment and rehabilitation.

CCIC 2010

The first edition of CCIC was launched with Bharath Ratna award winner and ace cricketer Kapil Dev as the brand ambassador. The highlight of the unique fundraiser was 'The Celebrity Golf Playoff" that took place at the 18th hole where 150 golfers participated. 'The celebrity golf playoff' itself was a custom-designed format that lasted about one hour, and was designed to entertain and give celebrities an opportunity to take a shot at the game.

Dr Vijay Anand, universally one of the top oncologists and founder of Cure Foundation said, "Through this event, we intend to raise money and spread cancer awareness in the society. Our objective is to create a consistent, annual, respectable charity golf tournament that will be well known in the golfer community."

It was a fun and fulfilling day of social service.

The Cancer Crusaders of CURE Foundation invite you to our exclusive biennial cancer awareness and fund raiser golf tournament.
